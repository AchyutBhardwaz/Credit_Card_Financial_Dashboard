\# Credit Card Financial Analysis



\## Overview

This is a analysis for a credit card company which wanted to see its analysed data to get informed insights.



\## Dataset

\- Source: From Youtube Tutorial (Rishabh Mishra)

\- Size: number of rows 10293 and columns 15

\- Period: 2026-06-15 to 2026-06-19



\## Analysis

\- Key analysis point 1

Target the Mid-Life Professional Segment: Marketing budgets should aggressively target individuals aged 40–60 who work as Businessmen or White-collar professionals, as they possess the highest transaction velocities and interest yields.



\- Key analysis point 2

Revamp Premium Card Offers: Since Platinum and Gold cards contribute under 7% of total revenue combined, consider auditing their reward incentives to capture higher-net-worth spenders. 



\- Key analysis point 3

Address Low CSAT: A satisfaction score of 3.19 is a warning indicator. The business should look into optimizing the dispute/resolution pathway, particularly on routine household charges like Bills and Groceries, which comprise the bulk of customer touchpoints.
