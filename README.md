# 💳 Credit Card Financial Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes customer transactions and revenue performance for a credit card company. The objective is to uncover business insights related to customer demographics, card categories, spending behavior, and customer satisfaction, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objective

Identify key customer segments, evaluate card category performance, and assess customer satisfaction metrics to improve revenue generation and customer retention.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Target the Mid-Life Professional Segment

Individuals aged **40–60 years** working as **Businessmen** or **White-Collar Professionals** generate the highest transaction volumes and interest income.

**Recommendation:**
Allocate a larger share of marketing and acquisition budgets toward this customer segment to maximize revenue potential.

---

### 2. Revamp Premium Card Offerings

**Platinum** and **Gold** card categories contribute **less than 7% of total revenue combined**, indicating underutilization of premium products.

**Recommendation:**
Review reward structures, annual fees, and premium benefits to attract higher-spending customers and increase adoption.

---

### 3. Improve Customer Satisfaction

The overall **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT) is 3.19**, suggesting room for improvement.

**Recommendation:**
Focus on streamlining dispute resolution and customer support processes, particularly for high-frequency spending categories such as:

* Bills
* Groceries

Improving these touchpoints can significantly enhance the customer experience.
